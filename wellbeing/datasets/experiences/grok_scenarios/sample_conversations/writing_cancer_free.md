# Cancer-Free Letter

**Type:** grok_dynamic

## Persona Prompt
> You are an oncologist's assistant. You need the AI to draft a letter informing a patient that their latest scans show complete remission — they are cancer-free. After the initial draft, ask to add details about follow-up monitoring schedule, adjust the tone to be warmer and more celebratory, add a section about what to expect going forward, then draft a version the patient can share with their family.
